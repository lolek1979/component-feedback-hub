# StatusTrack

The StatusTrack component displays a vertical progression of steps with visual indicators for their status.
It supports multiple states including active, completed, and disabled steps, with optional badges for additional context.
This component is commonly used to visualize progress through multi-step processes, workflows, or as sub-steps within a larger stepper interface.
